Our Mission

Seeing The Elephant: Originally a term for those who had seen battle, Seeing The Elephant is an interactive online experience focused on educating and informing viewers about the Civil war from the perspective of those who experienced it.

 

We feature the stories of the big names from the war such as Abraham Lincoln, Jefferson Davis, and Robert E. Lee. But we also seek to share the stories of people like Hugo Hildebrandt, a foreigner who took part in the war, Alfred Waud, a sketch artist who reported on battles, and Mary Chestnut, a confederate citizen who wrote extensively about the war's effect on her.
